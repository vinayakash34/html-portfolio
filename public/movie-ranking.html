The Best Movies According to Vinay

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Bareilly Ki Barfi

This is my favourite Bollywood Film. I love the acting of all the stars, especially Rajkumar Rao's acting.
His every transition from a macho man to a highly innocent guy is so smooth and funny. This movie's dialogues are always to the context of the situation and well written.

Soorarai Pottru

It's my source of energy and motivation, it's an inspiring and motivating film. Suriya Sir's has done justice to te character, he has lived that character to be honest.
It's wondeful film.

Dada

A feel good movie.
